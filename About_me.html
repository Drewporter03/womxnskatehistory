About Me

Natalie Porter

Thank you for visiting womxnskatehistory.ca
I began skateboarding in 1995 and wrote a thesis paper in 2003 called, Female Skateboarders and Their Negotiation of Space and Identity, before becoming a librarian in 2009. I have written and published a variety of articles, essays, and interviews on topics related to women in skateboarding, and continue to skate today. This website was launched on International Women’s Day, 2022.

Please contact me with content updates, edits, inquiries, or media requests via the form in the Conact page. Thank you for your support and acknowledgement.

- Natalie Porter
