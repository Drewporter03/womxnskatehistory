Womxn Skateboarding History

A Skateboarding History Collection

The goal of Womxnskatehistory is to create a digital database of stories and photos of female and non-binary skateboard legends.
Skateboarding has been around since the 1950s, when a genius put four wheels on a plank to get from surf spot to surf spot on the California coast (Becky, 2022).
Initially an underground sport, skateboarding rose to popularity in the 21st century, as people from all backgrounds came together to share their love for learning new tricks.
Males have always dominated the scene, comprising nearly 80% of all skateboarders (Isakov, 2021). Because of the unbalanced number of males to female skateboarders, the public has often left amazing female skaters out of the limelight, despite having incredible skills. For this reason, Natalie Porter, a skateboarder, librarian, motorcycle enthusiast, and published writer from Canada, created womxnskatehistory.ca.
