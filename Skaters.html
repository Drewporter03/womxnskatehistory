October 20, 2022 / 1980s Antonella Ferrero

Antonella Ferrero was an Italian slalom skater in the 1980s who won the Italian championship in 1980. She competed in the Junior division and either matched or surpassed Giammarco Luca, who became a legendary Italian skater in the 1990s, since they were the same age.
